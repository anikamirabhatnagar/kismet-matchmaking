Our Approach

We're living in a time where dating apps, algorithms, and filtered realities have reshaped how we connect. Swiping replaced serendipity. Performative small talk replaced genuine curiosity. In a world of endless options, many of us are left feeling lonelier than ever.

The result? A generation that's craving intimacy — but burnt out on the means to find it.

Our tailored approach helps you find a partner who genuinely aligns with your values, preferences, and long-term relationship goals. We ensure every client is ready for a committed relationship, because clarity and commitment are the foundations of a successful matchmaking journey.

Real Connection

Our matchmaking philosophy emphasises the importance of assessing your readiness for commitment and clarifying your vision of an ideal partner. This involves introspection about your personal goals, emotional readiness, and the qualities that truly matter to you.

Our Story

Kismet is founded by Mala in response to the growing need for intentional, relationship-focused matchmaking in today's world. We are focused on bringing back meaningful, intentional matchmaking in a world where genuine connection is increasingly rare.

Why We Chose the Name 'Kismet'

Kismet represents the beautiful belief that some connections are meant to be - that fate and destiny play a role in bringing the right people together at the right time.

Meet Mala - The Heart and Soul Behind Kismet

Mala is a natural "people person" and "the connector" who has spent over 30 years in the US building trusted networks within the South Asian community. Her authentic, honest, caring, and sincere approach has helped countless individuals find meaningful connections.

Our mission is simple: to help you find your person through intentional, committed relationships that honor compatibility, core values, love and understanding.
